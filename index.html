About Me

I am a passionate learner, classical dancer, and educator who believes in the power of creativity, discipline, and continuous growth. My journey has been shaped by my involvement in the performing arts, teaching young learners, and exploring the world of business and entrepreneurship.

I have trained extensively in Bharatanatyam and successfully completed my Arangetram, a milestone that represents years of dedication, discipline, and performance training. Alongside this, I am currently practicing Kathak, expanding my understanding of rhythm, storytelling, and expression.

I have performed in over 30 group stage performances and represented India at international cultural events in Turkey and Hungary. One of my most memorable experiences includes performing in front of Former Chief Minister of Gujarat, Vijay Rupani.

Alongside my artistic pursuits, I work as a Playgroup Teacher at Little Millennium School, where I create engaging and interactive learning environments for young children. I am also pursuing a BBA in Digital Business & Entrepreneurship from IIM Bangalore.
