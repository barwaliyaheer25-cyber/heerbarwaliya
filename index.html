About Me

I am a passionate learner, classical dancer, and educator who believes in creativity, discipline, and continuous growth. My journey has been shaped through performing arts, teaching, and business education.

I have trained in Bharatanatyam and completed my Arangetram, while currently practicing Kathak. Through dance, I have built strong stage presence, discipline, and storytelling ability.

I have performed in over 30 stage performances and represented India internationally in Turkey and Hungary. A memorable milestone was performing in front of Former Chief Minister Vijay Rupani.

Alongside this, I work as a Playgroup Teacher at Little Millennium School and pursue a BBA in Digital Business & Entrepreneurship from IIM Bangalore.
